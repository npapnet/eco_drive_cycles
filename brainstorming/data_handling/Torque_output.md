## G(x),  G(y), G(z)

These are acceleration values in the x, y, and z directions, respectively. they seem to be in units of m/s^2, and they represent the acceleration experienced by the vehicle in three-dimensional space.

**The values appear to be captured from the phone's accelerometer, which is typically used to measure the acceleration of the device in various directions.**: This means, that during the trip if the phone was moved or rotated in the car the values are not necessarily the acceleration of the car. **DO NOT TRUST THESE VALUES TO REPRESENT THE CAR'S ACCELERATION WITHOUT FURTHER VALIDATION.** They could be influenced by the phone's orientation, vibrations, or other factors unrelated to the car's actual movement.
